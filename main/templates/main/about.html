About Our School

Cachet Bearers Montessori Schools is dedicated to excellence in education, creativity, and personal growth.

Our Story

Founded in 2007, Cachet Bearers Montessori Schools has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region, providing quality education from nursery to primary levels. Our aim is to nurture confident, curious, and compassionate learners.
