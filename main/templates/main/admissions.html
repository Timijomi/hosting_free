Admissions

Join our community of excellence — where learning begins with love and discipline.

Admission Overview

At Cachet Bearers Montessori School, we welcome students into a nurturing environment designed to inspire creativity, confidence, and growth. Our admission process is straightforward and transparent.

Admission Procedure

  1. Obtain an admission form from the school office or website.
  2. Fill the form correctly and attach:
    • Two passport photographs
    • Birth certificate or declaration of age
    • Previous school results or transfer certificate
    • Medical fitness report
  3. Submit all documents to the admissions office.
  4. Attend an interview or entrance test as scheduled.
  5. Receive your admission letter upon successful evaluation.

Age Requirement

Nursery Section: 2 – 5 years old
Primary Section: 5 – 11 years old

Entrance Assessments

Every child seeking admission into our Nursery or Primary section must sit for an entrance assessment. This helps us evaluate academic readiness and place the student appropriately.

Important Dates

  • Admission Forms Available: January – August
  • Entrance Examination: March & June
  • Resumption for New Session: September
